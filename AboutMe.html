About Me

Professional Profile

Research, engineering practice and institutional experience integrated within a single technical path.

Andrea Vennarucci is a Civil Engineer and Ph.D. Candidate whose research focuses on road safety, driving simulation, virtual reality, and digital engineering methodologies for transportation systems.

His professional background combines engineering practice, public service and academic research, contributing to the development of innovative approaches for transportation safety, human factors analysis and digital infrastructure management.

His research interests include driving simulation, immersive virtual environments, artificial intelligence, digital twins, fire safety engineering and advanced technologies for mobility and infrastructure.

Transforming complex mobility challenges into innovative and data driven engineering solutions.
